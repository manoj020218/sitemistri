A Technician may upload one proof photo per site work upon marking work as completed. This photo:

Technician GPS location is collected only when the Technician actively sets their availability status to "Available Now", "Available Today", or "Available Tomorrow". Location is:

  • Only shared with a specific SI after that SI's site work has been accepted by the Technician
  • Not sold, rented, or shared with any third party for advertising purposes

11. Ratings & Trust Score

Trust scores are calculated based on completed work, unique SIs worked with, repeat engagements, ratings, and negative signals such as no-shows or cancellations after acceptance. Privacy Policy — SiteMitra

Effective Date: 5 June 2026  |  Last Updated: 5 June 2026
Developer: Jenix  |  Privacy Contact: privacy@iotsoft.com

1. Introduction

SiteMitra ("we", "our", "us"), developed by Jenix, is committed to protecting the privacy of all users ("you", "your"). This Privacy Policy explains what personal data we collect, why we collect it, how we use it, and your rights in relation to it.

By using the SiteMitra application or website, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Policy.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information (via Google Sign-In)

When you sign in using your Google Account, we receive:

  • Full name — displayed on your profile
  • Email address — used as your unique account identifier and for admin communications
  • Google Account ID — used internally to link your Google identity to your SiteMitra account
  • Profile photo URL — displayed on your profile (hosted by Google)
  • Email verification status — to ensure account quality

We do not receive your Google password. Authentication is handled entirely by Google's OAuth 2.0 infrastructure.

2.2 Profile Information (provided by you)

  • Mobile phone number — entered manually, used for SI contact and work coordination
  • City and working areas — used to match technicians with nearby SIs
  • Skills, experience level, and tools — used in discovery search and profile display
  • Vehicle type — used in profile and discovery
  • Business name and type (SI users only) — displayed on SI profile

2.3 Location Data (Technicians Only)

  • GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude), accuracy, and timestamp of last update
  • Collected only when a Technician explicitly sets availability to "Available Now", "Available Today", or "Available Tomorrow"
  • Not collected in the background — only on-demand when the user performs the availability update action
  • Stored in our database and updated each time the Technician updates their location via the app
  • Deleted when the user's account is deleted

2.4 Proof Photos

  • One photo per completed site work, uploaded voluntarily by the Technician
  • Resized and converted to WebP format (max 1200×900 px) before storage
  • Stored in a temporary server-side directory — not publicly accessible by URL
  • Permanently deleted within 7 days of upload, or immediately when the SI closes the site work — whichever comes first
  • A SHA-256 hash of the file is retained for record-keeping after the file is deleted

2.5 Push Notification Tokens

  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM) device token, collected when you enable notifications
  • Device platform (web, Android, or iOS)
  • Used solely to deliver push notifications about site work events
  • Deleted when you log out or when Firebase marks the token as invalid

2.6 Terms Acceptance Record

  • IP address at the time you accepted the Terms and Privacy Policy
  • User-agent string of your browser/device at that time
  • Timestamp of acceptance
  • Version of Terms and Privacy Policy accepted

This is stored for legal compliance purposes only.

2.7 Usage Data

  • Last active timestamp — updated on each authenticated API request
  • We do not use third-party analytics SDKs, advertising networks, or behavioural tracking tools

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To create, authenticate, and manage your SiteMitra account
  • To enable SIs to discover nearby available Technicians based on location and skills
  • To create and manage site work assignments between SIs and Technicians
  • To calculate and display trust scores based on verified completed work
  • To send push notifications for site work events (new assignment, status changes, reminders)
  • To generate an auto-written profile bio in Hindi and English based on your skills
  • To enforce our Terms of Service (e.g., detecting and blocking policy violators)
  • To provide admin tools for platform moderation

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. We share your data only in the following limited circumstances:

4.1 With Other Users (within the Platform)

  • A Technician's name, city, skills, availability, trust score, and profile photo are visible to searching SIs
  • A Technician's exact location is shared with a specific SI only after that SI's site work has been accepted
  • An SI's name and business name are visible to assigned Technicians
  • Mobile numbers are shared only between parties in an active site work assignment

4.2 With Third-Party Service Providers

  • Google (Firebase) — for push notification delivery via FCM
  • Google LLC — for authentication via Google Sign-In / OAuth 2.0
  • Google Fonts — for web font delivery (no personal data shared)

These providers are bound by their own privacy policies. We do not share your data with any advertising networks or data brokers.

4.3 Legal Disclosure

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law, court order, or in response to a legitimate request by law enforcement authorities under Indian law (including the Information Technology Act, 2000 and its amendments).

5. Data Retention

  • Account data — retained while your account is active; deleted within 30 days of a verified account deletion request
  • Location coordinates — overwritten with each update; deleted on account deletion
  • Proof photos — maximum 7 days; deleted immediately upon work closure or expiry
  • FCM tokens — deleted on logout; auto-removed when Firebase marks them as invalid
  • Terms acceptance record — retained for legal compliance even after account deletion

6. Data Security

We implement the following security measures to protect your data:

  • All data transmitted between the app and our servers is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS
  • Authentication uses JWT tokens with a 30-day expiry; no passwords are stored
  • Database access is restricted to our application server — no direct public access
  • Proof photos are stored server-side in a non-public directory and are not accessible via a guessable public URL
  • Rate limiting is applied to all API endpoints to prevent abuse
  • HTTP security headers (via Helmet.js) are enforced on all responses

While we take reasonable precautions, no method of internet transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

As a user of SiteMitra, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a summary of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — update inaccurate information directly via the app's profile settings
  • Deletion — request deletion of your account and all associated personal data by contacting privacy@iotsoft.com. Deletion will be completed within 30 days.
  • Location withdrawal — stop location sharing at any time by setting your availability to "Offline" in the app
  • Notification opt-out — disable push notifications via your device settings at any time

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@iotsoft.com.

8. Children's Privacy

SiteMitra is intended for professionals aged 18 and above. We do not knowingly collect personal data from users under 18 years of age. If we become aware that a user is under 18, their account will be deleted promptly. If you believe a minor has used the Platform, please notify us immediately at privacy@iotsoft.com.
